Daniel (Prime)

"have a danieltastic day!" - Daniel (Prime)

DANIEL Middlename Cridland (also known by 'D. Criddy' or simply 'Cridders') is the level 500 yakuza boss right-hand man to the infamous Billius 'billy' Baker, whomst he is Best Friends with.

He is most well known for creating the JHC (Judy Hopps Chronicles), the internet's most disgusting, horrible, webcomic; creating Aphantasia, Steam's hottest, freshest, youngest indie game made in rpgmaker; and notable feats such as 'most babies eaten in one sitting', 'most respectful japanese bows in 1 hour', and of course 'most irish'.

Daniel would lead an ordinary life until he would apply to UCM (University College Isle of Man) and meet his lesser friends. He would take this oppurtunity to reinvent himself into a more maniacal, cold, unfeeling, dominant force that would shackle his new found 'lesser friends' and sought for their misery. In time, he would find this misery as a source of inspiration to create more creative media and fuel more world building into his existing works.
