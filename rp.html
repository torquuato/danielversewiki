DANIELVERSE RP RULES

  1. Don't use inappropriate language! Swearing is allowed but keep sensitive themes to a minimum please.
  2. Please keep the chat relevant to serious RP only.
  3. If you want to leave the current RP, please do the courtesy of informing people you are leaving (e.g. [user is leaving RP] )
  4. Please use tone tags when you think a message could be misconstrued into a different tonal context (e.g. 'i think daniel prime is poo! /j )
  5. Just be freakin sensible

Do '!help' for the basic rundown.
